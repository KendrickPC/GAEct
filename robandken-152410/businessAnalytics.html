Business Analytics

Learning to make data-driven business decisions.

Business Analytics Course Desctription

Learn about advanced data analytics without writing a single line of code. Through this program, you'll master the most important predictive skills and tools, and you won't need to know programming to do it!

We provide licenses to two powerful analytics and data visualization tools, Alteryx Designer and Tableau Desktop. These software licenses are worth over $2500 USD.

The second half of our course provides an introduction to big data analytics for all business professionals, including those with no prior analytics experience. You’ll learn how data analysts describe, predict, and inform business decisions in the specific areas of marketing, human resources, finance, and operations, and you’ll develop basic data literacy and an analytic mindset that will help you make strategic decisions based on data. In the final Capstone Project, you’ll apply your skills to interpret a real-world data set and make appropriate business strategy recommendations.


Proposal Description:

The first half of this course is co-created by alterYX and Tableau. It requires approximately 160 hours to complete. No prior coding experience is needed. When finished, students will be certified with a nanodegree from Udacity.

The second half of this course focuses is created by the University of Pennsylvania, Wharton School of Business. This course requires 4 weeks of study with approximately 5 to 6 hours per week. Also, no prior coding experience is needed to complete this course. When finished, students will be certified by the University of Pennsylvania, Wharton School of Business.
