OUR MISSION

Our mission is to make learning to code easy and accessible to students of all ages and language backgrounds. All coding is done in English. We recognize that there is a knowledge gap both in understanding the latest technology as well as in language. We aim to identify where these areas exist and offer learning solutions that bolster confidence and inspire student development.
